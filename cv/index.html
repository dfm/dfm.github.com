I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Center for Cosmology & Particle Physics at New York University working with David W. Hogg.

I'm interested in approximate probabilistic inference, exoplanet observations and populations, and data analysis in general. I mostly work with large public datasets in astronomy. I also contribute to and maintain some open source projects on GitHub.
